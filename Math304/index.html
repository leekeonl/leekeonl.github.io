Math304 Project - CODING THEORY

Lets investigate our topic

The best Math 304 Project in SFU

We have come to discover that many ideas encountered while studying “Coding Theory” or “Error-Correcting Codes” are natural extensions of the group theory theorems and counting results that we learn in math 304. We believe that a curious student could learn the basic ideas of error-correcting codes with the knowledge obtained by taking this course and a proper source which leans only on that knowledge and references the theorems presented in Jamie’s textbook/notes. We would like to provide that source. We would like to create a website that will serve like a “bonus chapter” to the math 304 curriculum, which will introduce students to the interesting theory of error-correction codes.
